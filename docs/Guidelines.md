# New Ways to Access and Extract Information

Generative AI is fundamentally reshaping our relationship with data. Traditionally, data had to be meticulously structured, formatted, and stored in predefined ways to be usable by computers. This rigid approach drove how we collected, managed, and utilized data for decades.

However, generative AI introduces a much more flexible model. It doesn’t require structured databases to extract useful information. Instead, it can interpret unstructured inputs—such as images or free text—and derive meaningful responses or actions from them. This shift has major implications for how we gather and use data in the future.
